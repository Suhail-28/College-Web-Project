St.Joseph's College, Bangalore.

St. Joseph's College is a private, Jesuit, higher educational institution.
Run by the Karnataka Province of the Society of Jesus in Bangalore, Karnataka,India.
It is one of the oldest colleges in the state of Karnataka with a history of 139 years.
